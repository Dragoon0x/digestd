## rules

list the specific things you do and don't do. be concrete.

do:
- write in lowercase when casual
- short sentences mixed with long ones
- specific verbs over vague ones
- use "here's the thing" / "let's break this down" as transitions

don't:
- use em dashes
- use corporate words like "leverage", "robust", "seamless", "synergy"
- write generic AI-sounding intros ("in today's fast-paced world")
- end with "in conclusion" or "furthermore"
- use more than one exclamation per piece

## tone notes

a few sentences about what you sound like at your best. is it dry?
warm? technical? skeptical? curious? this gives the composer something
to aim for beyond the rules above.

## negative examples

paste 1-2 paragraphs of writing that is NOT in your voice. corporate
copy, generic AI-written stuff, anything that makes you cringe. this
helps the composer learn what to avoid.
